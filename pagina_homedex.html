Here is a list of the top 3 best destinations suggested by our users

Papeete, French Polynesia

A gracious tropical city with tall palms and abundant flowers, Papeete lies on the northwest coast of Tahiti and is one of the largest urban centres in the South Pacific. Its excellent harbour made it, by the 1830s, a place of trade and a favourite port of call for whalers.

Braies Lake, Italy

Lake Braies is an attraction for recreation seekers, nature lovers and hikers, in both summer and winter. The lake, with an average depth of 17 m, measures 36 m at its lowest point. Even though Braies lake is listed amongst the bathing lakes of South Tyrol, only a few hardy people dare to jump into the cool waters.

Madeira, Portugal

Madeira, Portugal; a holiday and tourism guide for 2023. Madeira is an oasis of green within the Atlantic Ocean. This lush island with its permanent spring-like climate, is diverse and fascinating, and one of the truly unique holiday destinations of Europe
