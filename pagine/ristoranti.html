BEST RESTAURANTS IN SANTORINI, GREECE

Iriana Cafe, Santorini

The Iriana Cafe is open all day and is waiting for you to enjoy the authentic Santorini experience in an elegant environment. On a beautiful terrace overlooking the Caldera and the endless blue of the Aegean Sea, sitting in comfortable colourful seats, listening to selected music, enjoying cool drinks and delicious recipes, with the fresh breeze bringing you the aroma of the Aegean. you will simply relax and enjoy the stunning view. A unique experience that will keep in your memory.

Tholotho Brunch and Restaurant, Santorini

Tholotho Brunch and Restaurant is a cozy and charming eatery located in the heart of Santorini, Greece. The restaurant specializes in brunch-style dishes with a creative and contemporary twist, as well as traditional Greek cuisine using fresh and locally sourced ingredients. The restaurant's interior is decorated in a rustic and bohemian style, with a warm and inviting atmosphere that makes it a popular spot for both locals and tourists. The outdoor seating area offers stunning views of the island and the Aegean Sea, making it the perfect place to enjoy a meal while taking in the beautiful surroundings.

Le Moustache Caldera Pool Lounge & Restaurant, Santorini

Thanks its wonderful pool and internals Le Moustache has been one of the most selected restaurants of our site. Our utents are always pleased by the service offered and for the high level menu proposals.

Alisachni art and wine gallery, Santorini

"A traditional place which aged more than 200 years where you can see handmade art from Greek sculptures. At the same time you can enjoy wines from all over Greece join them with Greek tastes."
